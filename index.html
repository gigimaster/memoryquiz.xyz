How to Play

Step 1: Observe

A number will appear on your screen for 5 seconds. Focus and try to memorize it.

Step 2: Remember

The number will disappear after 5 seconds. Keep it in your memory.

Step 3: Input

Type the number you remembered into the input box and submit your answer.

Step 4: Progress

Move through all 20 levels, with each level adding one more digit to remember.

Scoring System

How Points Are Calculated

• Level 1: 5 points

• Level 2: 10 points

• Level 3: 15 points

• And so on, with each level worth 5 more points than the previous

• Maximum possible score: 1050 points

Performance Ratings

• Excellent: 840+ points (80% or higher)

• Good: 630-835 points (60-79%)

• Practice Needed: Below 630 points (Below 60%)
